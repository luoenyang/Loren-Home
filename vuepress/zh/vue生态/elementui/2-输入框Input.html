input搜索输入框

扩展了搜索按钮/回车搜索

# html

<span class="searchBox">
  <el-input
    @keyup.enter.native="doInputSearch()"
    @clear="doInputSearch()"
    :clearable="true"
    placeholder="请输入标题关键字"
    v-model="searchText"
  >
    <template #append>
      <span class="searchBt" @click="doInputSearch">搜索</span>
    </template>
  </el-input>
</span>
